Quality control and quality assurance management

KetSoftware IT quality assurance specialists have solid experience in both manual and automated types of QA testing

Manual Testing

This approach to quality control and quality assurance is based on running your software on various devices. Our specialists also pay much attention to the convenience and usability of the provided product. The main benefits of manual testing:

  • A manual quality assurance tester can detect some issues that cannot be identified during automated software assessment.
  • This approach provides a better understanding of a product's vulnerabilities and issues, which may set a clearer direction for its optimization.
  • A manual quality assurance tester provides relevant feedback about the usability of a newly developed product and this software's user experience.

Automated Testing

Such an approach allows reducing the human impact on the product. Automated software testing and quality assurance is more objective and attentive to details since it covers all possible issues that may disrupt software functionality. It also expands functionalities of manual testing, which sets both types of product quality assurance as a proper combination. The main benefits of automation quality assurance are the following:

  • Automated quality assurance in software development life cycle allows saving time required for the completion of repetitive tasks.
  • Such quality assurance services are more objective, which eliminates human error.
  • The basic functionalities of automated testing services are adapted to align with manual testing processes.
